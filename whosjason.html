Who Am I

I'm Jason Truong, a first year computer science student at Ryerson University in Toronto, Canada.

I became interested in computer science from a young age. In grade 4 I was hooked on flash games and coding objects into Roblox...but with little success

This attraction would lay dormant until grade 11 when I took a computer science course. This would unlock my childhood fascination, and for the first time, I was enjoying school!

Now, if all goes well, I'll be graduating with a BSc. Hons degree in Computer Science in 2025 as I'm enrolled in a 5 year coop program.

Activites

Ryerson's Computer Science Course Union

The Ryerson CSCU is a group of elected students that aim to provide computer science students with the best opportunities and experience.

As the Deputy of VP Operations, I perform tasks that range from planning events/workshops to voicing the concerns of CS students to the CS department heads

The CSCU also collaborates with student groups to further provide students with larger scale workshops. One example was an event where I, on behalf of the CSCU, collaborated with wiCS (Women in CS), LUG (Linux User Group), DSC (Developer Student Club), and Guelph and McMaster University to host "Technical Interview Day"

At this event, we invited 5 ex-FAANG Ryerson, Guelph, and McMaster students to talk about the interview process and the tips and tricks to acing the Technical Interview!
