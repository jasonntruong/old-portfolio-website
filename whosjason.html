Who Am I

I'm Jason Truong, a Vietnamese first year computer science student at Ryerson University in Toronto, Canada.

My interest in computer science sparked at a young age. In grade 4 I was hooked on coding flash games and objects into Roblox...but with little success

My obsession would lay dormant until I took a computer science course in grade 11. This would soon re-kindle my childhood fascination, and for the first time, I was enjoying school!

Fast forward to today, if all goes well, I'll be graduating with a BSc. Hons degree in Computer Science in 2025 as I'm enrolled in a 5 year coop program.

Activites

Ryerson's Computer Science Course Union

The Ryerson CSCU is a group of elected students that aim to provide computer science students with the best opportunities and experience.

As the Deputy of VP Operations, I perform tasks that range from planning events/workshops to voicing the concerns of CS students to the CS department heads

The CSCU also collaborates with student groups to further provide students with larger scale workshops. One example was an event where I, on behalf of the CSCU, collaborated with wiCS (Women in CS), LUG (Linux User Group), DSC (Developer Student Club), and Guelph and McMaster University to host "Technical Interview Day"

At this event, we invited 5 ex-FAANG Ryerson, Guelph, and McMaster students to talk about the interview process and the tips and tricks to acing the Technical Interview!

Online Freelance Tutor

At the start of the school year, I decided to start online tutoring since I did not work during the Summer due to covid.

I tutor elementary school students via Zoom. My responsibilites include helping my students with their homework, re-explaining key concepts from class, assigning homework for practice, and answering questions via email off hours.

Ultimately, since starting in September 2020, I have been responsible for the academic success of my students. :D
